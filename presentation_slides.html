The Problem

Ecological Crisis

  • Green crabs: World's worst invasive species
  • Destroying native shellfish populations
  • $20M annual damage to US fisheries

Economic Opportunity

  • Soft-shell crab delicacy market
  • $30-50 per pound retail value
  • Critical: 2-3 day harvest window

Challenge: Must harvest within 72 hours before molting or lose entire value

Green Crab Molting Cycle

Molt Stages:

  1. Inter-molt (>14 days): Active feeding
  2. Pre-molt (4-14 days): Preparation phase
  3. Peeler (0-3 days): HARVEST WINDOW
  4. Ecdysis (2-3 hours): Active molting
  5. Post-molt (weeks): Shell hardening

Visual Indicators: Color progression Green ‚Üí Yellow ‚Üí Orange ‚Üí Red

Research Question

Can computer vision predict molt timing accurately enough for commercial harvesting?

Success Criteria: <2 day prediction error

Approach: Compare single-shot vs. temporal models

Dataset Overview

Collection

  • 230 total images
  • 11 crabs (9F, 2M)
  • 4-month observation period
  • Time-series photography

Challenges

  • 81.7% gender imbalance
  • 39% samples in 0-5 day range
  • Irregular observation intervals
  • Variable lighting conditions
